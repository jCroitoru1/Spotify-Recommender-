Music Genre Intelligence Dashboard

A static, GitHub Pages-ready analytics experience built from Spotify artist exports. It compares popularity, artist depth, and genre crossover in a way that feels closer to a lightweight data product than a notebook.

Dataset 7 genres, client-side parsed CSVs, zero build step
